**Status**: Demo video temporarily unavailable due to hosting issues.

## Quick Demo Steps

1. **Login**: Use admin/admin123 to access the system
2. **Dashboard**: View sales statistics and quick actions
3. **Products**: Browse and manage inventory with category filters
4. **Sales**: Process transactions with barcode scanning
5. **Customers**: Manage customer profiles and loyalty
6. **Reports**: Generate sales and inventory reports
7. **Settings**: Configure branding, themes, and integrations

## Key Features Demonstrated

- **Offline-first operation**: Works without internet connection
- **White-label branding**: Customizable company colors and logos
- **Multi-tenant support**: Isolated data per business
- **Role-based permissions**: Different access levels for staff
- **Real-time sync**: Automatic data synchronization between stores
