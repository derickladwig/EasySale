# Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to EasySale

This guide will help you get started with the CAPS Point of Sale system. Whether you're a cashier, inventory clerk, or manager, this guide covers the basics you need to know.

## Logging In

1. **Open the EasySale application** on your desktop
2. **Enter your username and password**
3. **Click "Login"**

Your username and password are provided by your manager. If you've forgotten your password, contact your manager to reset it.

## Understanding Your Role

Your role determines what features you can access:

- **Cashier**: Process sales, returns, and exchanges
- **Parts Specialist**: Look up parts, check inventory, create special orders
- **Paint Tech**: Mix paint, look up formulas, manage paint inventory
- **Inventory Clerk**: Receive stock, adjust inventory, perform counts
- **Manager**: All of the above plus reports and settings
- **Admin**: Full system access including user management

## Processing a Sale (Cashiers)

### Basic Sale

1. **Click "Sell" in the left navigation**
2. **Scan the product barcode** or search for the product
3. **Product appears in the cart** on the right side
4. **Repeat for all items**
5. **Click "Payment"** when ready to checkout
6. **Select payment method** (Cash, Card, etc.)
7. **Process payment** and **print receipt**

### Applying a Discount

1. **Add items to cart**
2. **Click the discount icon** next to the item
3. **Enter discount amount or percentage**
4. **Click "Apply"**

Note: Large discounts may require manager approval.

### Processing a Return

1. **Click "Sell" in the left navigation**
2. **Click "Return" button** at the top
3. **Scan the product barcode** or search for the product
4. **Enter quantity to return**
5. **Select return reason** (defective, wrong item, etc.)
6. **Process refund** and **print receipt**

## Looking Up Products

### Search by Barcode

1. **Click "Lookup" in the left navigation**
2. **Scan the barcode** or type it in the search box
3. **Product details appear** including:
   - Name and description
   - Price and stock level
   - Location in store
   - Alternative products

### Search by Keyword

1. **Click "Lookup" in the left navigation**
2. **Type keywords** in the search box (e.g., "red cap", "oil filter")
3. **Browse results** and click to see details

### Search by Category

**For Caps**:
- Filter by size (One Size, S, M, L, XL)
- Filter by color
- Filter by brand

**For Auto Parts**:
- Enter make, model, and year
- System shows compatible parts
- Filter by OEM or aftermarket

**For Paint**:
- Enter paint code or color name
- System shows formula and tint codes
- Check stock of base and tints

## Receiving Stock (Inventory Clerks)

1. **Click "Warehouse" in the left navigation**
2. **Click "Receive Stock"**
3. **Scan or enter purchase order number**
4. **Scan each item** as you unpack it
5. **Verify quantity** matches packing slip
6. **Click "Complete Receipt"**
7. **Print labels** for shelves if needed

## Adjusting Inventory

1. **Click "Warehouse" in the left navigation**
2. **Click "Adjust Inventory"**
3. **Scan or search for product**
4. **Enter new quantity**
5. **Select reason** (damaged, lost, found, etc.)
6. **Add notes** explaining the adjustment
7. **Click "Save"**

Note: All inventory adjustments are logged and reviewed by managers.

## Working Offline

EasySale works 100% offline. If your internet connection is lost:

1. **Offline indicator appears** in the top bar
2. **Continue working normally** - all features still work
3. **Changes are saved locally** and will sync when connection returns
4. **Don't worry** - no data is lost

When the connection returns:
- **Online indicator appears** in the top bar
- **Changes sync automatically** to other stores
- **No action required** from you

## Viewing Reports (Managers)

1. **Click "Reports" in the left navigation**
2. **Select report type**:
   - Daily Sales Summary
   - Inventory Levels
   - Employee Performance
   - Customer Activity
3. **Select date range**
4. **Click "Generate Report"**
5. **View on screen** or **print/export**

## Common Tasks

### Checking Stock Levels

1. **Click "Lookup"**
2. **Search for product**
3. **Stock level shown** in product details
4. **Red badge** indicates low stock

### Creating a Quote

1. **Click "Sell"**
2. **Add items to cart**
3. **Click "Save as Quote"** instead of Payment
4. **Enter customer information**
5. **Print quote** for customer
6. **Quote can be converted to sale later**

### Managing Layaways

1. **Click "Sell"**
2. **Add items to cart**
3. **Click "Layaway"**
4. **Enter customer information**
5. **Collect deposit**
6. **Print layaway receipt**

To complete a layaway:
1. **Click "Sell"**
2. **Click "Layaway Pickup"**
3. **Enter layaway number** or search by customer
4. **Collect remaining balance**
5. **Complete sale**

### Voiding a Transaction

1. **Click "Reports"**
2. **Click "Recent Transactions"**
3. **Find the transaction** to void
4. **Click "Void"**
5. **Enter reason** for void
6. **Confirm**

Note: Voiding transactions requires manager approval.

## Tips for Success

1. **Scan barcodes when possible** - faster and more accurate than typing
2. **Use keyboard shortcuts** - saves time on repetitive tasks
3. **Check stock before promising** - avoid disappointing customers
4. **Keep your password secure** - never share with others
5. **Log out when leaving** - protects customer and business data
6. **Report issues immediately** - helps us fix problems quickly

## Troubleshooting

### Barcode Scanner Not Working

1. **Check USB connection** - unplug and replug
2. **Test scanner** - scan a barcode from a product
3. **Restart application** if still not working
4. **Contact IT support** if problem persists

### Printer Not Printing

1. **Check printer power** - ensure it's turned on
2. **Check paper** - ensure paper is loaded correctly
3. **Check USB connection** - unplug and replug
4. **Restart printer** - turn off and on
5. **Contact IT support** if problem persists

### Application Running Slow

1. **Close unused windows** - reduces memory usage
2. **Restart application** - clears temporary data
3. **Check offline status** - sync may be running
4. **Contact IT support** if problem persists

### Can't Find a Product

1. **Try different search terms** - use brand name, SKU, or description
2. **Check spelling** - typos prevent matches
3. **Browse by category** - use filters to narrow down
4. **Ask manager** - product may not be in system yet
